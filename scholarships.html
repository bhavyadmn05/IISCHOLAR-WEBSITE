Netherlands

Orange Tulip Scholarship

Funded by Dutch universities for international students, offering world-class education in one of Europe's most innovative academic environments.

Benefits
  • Partial to full tuition coverage
  • Monthly stipend (varies by institution)
  • Access to Dutch university networks
Eligibility: International students from eligible countries; requirements vary by university partner.
Apply Now

Japan

MEXT Scholarship

Japan's prestigious government scholarship — one of the most comprehensive fully funded programs for international students pursuing undergraduate or postgraduate studies.

Benefits
  • Full tuition waiver
  • Monthly stipend (¥117,000–¥144,000)
  • Round-trip airfare covered
Eligibility: Applicants under 35 years; strong academic record; Japanese or English language proficiency.
Apply Now
🇬🇧
United Kingdom

Commonwealth Scholarship

The UK government's flagship international scholarship for citizens of Commonwealth nations, fostering leadership and knowledge exchange across the globe.

Benefits
  • Full tuition at UK universities
  • Living allowance & stipend
  • Return travel to the UK
Eligibility: Citizens of Commonwealth countries; Bachelor's degree (2:1 or above); commitment to development.
Apply Now
🇺🇸
United States

Fulbright Scholarship

The world's most prestigious exchange program, funded by the US government, empowering future leaders through academic excellence and cross-cultural understanding.

Benefits
  • Full tuition at US institutions
  • Monthly living stipend
  • Return airfare & health insurance
Eligibility: Bachelor's degree holders; strong academic and professional record; English proficiency required.
Apply Now
🇪🇺
Europe

Erasmus Mundus Joint Master

An elite EU-funded program offering fully funded master's degrees across multiple prestigious European universities — a truly international academic experience.

Benefits
  • Full tuition across partner universities
  • Monthly stipend up to €1,400
  • Travel & installation allowances
Eligibility: Bachelor's degree; open to all nationalities; strong academic standing and motivation letter.
Apply Now
