Professional Journey

Counselling & Mentorship

Over the course of my professional journey, I have counselled and mentored over 5,000 students, supporting them across Bachelor's, Master's, and doctoral (PhD) pathways. My approach extends beyond admissions guidance to emphasise academic alignment, long-term outcomes, and student well-being.

I have worked across organisations such as Turito and Scholist, contributing to student counselling, academic guidance, and structured global education pathways. I have also supported students in research engagement and academic writing, including contributions to published research work, encouraging critical thinking and academic depth.

I founded Study Abroad – IISPPR to create a platform that integrates ethical counselling, mentorship, and policy awareness, supporting students in becoming globally aware and academically grounded learners.

Social Initiative

Seher – The Dawn
of Empowerment

Seher works to support girls in Afghanistan in accessing and returning to education, with a focus on career awareness, educational continuity, and long-term empowerment.

The initiative is grounded in the belief that education is essential to agency, dignity, and opportunity — particularly in contexts where access is limited.

Through Seher, efforts are directed towards enabling pathways back to learning, confidence-building, and sustained educational engagement, aligned with broader goals of gender equity and inclusive development.
